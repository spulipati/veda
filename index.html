OVER VIEW

Founded in 2013, Veda Distribution is a niche player in IT and Networking Infrastructure Distribution providing Value Added Distribution Service in Networking, Automation and Electronic Security Products in Andhra, Telanga and Karnataka States with a revenue run rate of 2 crores. Veda has a direct relationship with 4 Global Technology vendors & is headquartered in Vijayawada with a presence at Hyderabad & Bangalore

Veda is a lean organization with an effective and efficient team. The Promoter team members have a collective relevant industry experience to offer distribution solutions to our customers.
