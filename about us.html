Veda Distribution : An Overview
  • Founded in 2005, Veda Distribution is a niche player in IT Infrastructure Distribution & Services
  • Value Added Distributor for Information Security, Networking, Automatic Identification and Data Capture & POS, Infrastructure & Application Software, Storage Management, Electronic Security products & related Services in South Asia
  • Direct relationship with 40 Global Technology vendors
  • Headquartered in Bangalore with presence in 16 locations.
  • Veda Distribution Group operates through 4 Business Units
  • Total team strength of 179 including 20% technical (SE and FE)
  • Promoter team members have a collective relevant industry experience of 200+ man-years.
  • Business done through 700+ channel partners
  • One or more solutions offered to 6000+ end customers

Business Model

Shareholding
  • Promoted in 2005, Veda Distribution was largely held by employee shareholders
  • Veda Distribution received an investment from Datatec/ Westcon Group in September 2008 and formed a JV
  • Veda Distribution formed a wholly owned subsidiary Westcon India in Jan 2009 to focus on AIDC & other complementing businesses
  • Comstor BU was introduced in Mar 2010 upon starting Cisco relationship in India / South Asia
  • Management & operations continued to be with Veda team as defined in JV agreement
  • Discussions in Jan 2012 by Veda promoters to buy back Westcon Group's equity in Veda
  • SPA signed by promoters' with Westcon Group on 24th July'13 and closed on 31st August '13
  • The core Veda Distribution team remains the same and continues to drive the business
