WHY VEDA


Our Role

The Business model of Veda Distribution is an indirect model and an extension of the OEM and act as their link to a System Integrator and the End-Customer. Veda Distribution engage in enabling our partners to deliver new technologies to their customers and enable end customers to adopt new technologies.

As part of customer enablement activities, we work closely with OEMs & partners to conduct various marketing activities, road shows, events and seminars to educate customers on new technologies and encourage them to adopt the same.

Our Core Competencies
  • We carry Networking, IT infrastructure, Security Surveillance products of major OEM’s and offering those products in large volumes to our customers and actively engaging in retail markets.
  • Is to Build and develop partnership with OEM’s and customers founded on trust, competitive pricing and consistent quality of service
  • Distributing Branded products to System Integrators and Retail Markets.
  • Differentiating ourselves by providing the best customer service
  • Valuing long term relation with suppliers and Customers.
  • Maintaining a vision towards the future to take lead in new technology products while making out commitment to quality of service.
  • Focused on delivering a premium experience through superior service for our clients.
  • The pursuit and achievement of our mission will result in sustainable growth for us, OEM’s and system integrators. Together, we all win.
